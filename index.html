About Us

Suntech Engineers & Contractors (SEC) is a pioneer in innovative workforce solutions. At SEC, we are dedicated to helping our clients achieve their business objectives with employees that fit their culture and bring a high level of talent and innovation. Our workforce solutions include placing unskilled, semi-skilled, skilled, and highly skilled personnel in different industry verticals across the Middle East and Gulf countries.

We believe that in today’s economy, having a diverse team of employees is more important than ever. Our team of highly qualified and experienced recruiters understands the value of a diverse workforce and has made over 500,000 placements in various industries with over 750 satisfied clients.

We build bridges between talent management and business processes by providing clients with innovative, customizable workforce services that support their success.

Suntech Engineers and Contractors (SEC), was founded in 1990 by Mr. Sunil Chander Sharma, a Civil Engineer graduate from DCE. His vision was to contribute to the world’s growth, which was already in progress. With his visionary leadership, SEC was ranked 5th in India by the Ministry of External Affairs.
