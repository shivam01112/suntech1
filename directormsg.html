Chairman's Message

Chairman’s Message

Suntech Engineers and Contractors (SEC), was founded in 1990 by Mr. Sunil Chander Sharma, a Civil Engineer graduate from DCE. His vision was to contribute to the world’s growth, which was already in progress. With his visionary leadership, SEC was ranked 5th in India by the Ministry of External Affairs.

Accompanying Mr. Sunil Chander Sharma in carrying forward his vision are his sons—Mr. Shivvang Sharma and Mr. Paraaj Sunil Sharma.

Mr. Shivvang Sharma began his journey at Suntech as an intern during his academic years, deeply aligned with the mission of creating livelihood opportunities and supporting global workforce development.

Mr. Paraaj Sunil Sharma, with a strong background in the mining and infrastructure sector, is now actively involved in the operations and strategic growth of Suntech Engineers & Contractors, contributing his expertise to further strengthen the company’s presence in the global recruitment industry.

Together, they represent the next generation of leadership—blending innovation with legacy to carry Suntech’s vision forward.

Over the past 35 years, SEC has had the privilege of providing skilled manpower to some of the most prestigious projects worldwide, like Burj Khalifa, Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, Palm Jumeirah Island, Burj Al Arab, MIG Tower, as well as collaborations with renowned organizations such as Arabian Construction Company, Urbacon Trading and Contracting, Fibrex, NPCC, and many more, spanning various industries including Oil and Gas, Construction, Healthcare, and Hospitality.

We take pride in our ability to source talented individuals from countries like India, Nepal, Bangladesh, and more, contributing to the development of nations across the globe. Our headquarters are located in India, and we also have a strong presence in Doha and Dubai, boasting a team of over 100 experts dedicated to our mission of addressing the global manpower shortage with high-quality candidates.

Throughout our journey, we have successfully delivered a workforce exceeding 500,000 individuals and fostered partnerships with over 750 satisfied clients. Our track record includes effectively providing manpower to countries such as Saudi Arabia, Oman, Iraq, UAE, Bahrain, Kuwait, Africa and beyond. At Suntech Engineers and Contractors, our commitment to excellence remains unwavering as we continue to play a pivotal role in the world’s development and growth.

Why Choose Us

Connecting Talent, Creating Opportunities

We connect the right talent with the right opportunities—through personalized service and deep industry knowledge—to ensure every placement delivers real impact.

Suntech Engineers & Contractors was born out of a real-world challenge: the critical disconnect between job roles and candidate capabilities. While serving as a Project Manager in the construction industry, I witnessed firsthand how poor recruitment led to project delays, safety issues, and increased costs.

To solve this, I established Suntech in 1990, building a team of expert engineers and supervisors with deep domain knowledge across Oil & Gas, EPC, MEP, Facility Management, and more.

Our mission is to bridge the talent gap by identifying, screening, and deploying the right workforce with speed and precision—delivering reliability and results to businesses worldwide.

To empower skilled workers and connect them with the right opportunities while preserving the legacy of integrity, commitment, and service excellence built by our founder. Inspired by our father’s entrepreneurial spirit and guided by a technical foundation, We aim to uphold and evolve the Suntech legacy—supporting the livelihoods of thousands and becoming a trusted name in global manpower solutions.

We don’t just fill vacancies—we build teams that perform.

Through a structured recruitment process, industry-specific assessments, and a dedicated operations team, we ensure that only qualified and job-ready professionals are matched with employers.

From blue-collar trades to specialized engineers, our manpower solutions are tailored for sectors that demand skill, compliance, and efficiency.

With over three decades of experience, Suntech offers:
  • Pre-screened & tested manpower
  • End-to-end project-based staffing
  • Customized recruitment based on client needs
  • Global reach with local expertise
