Chairman's Message

Chairman’s Message

Suntech Engineers & Contractors (SEC) was founded in 1990 by Mr. Sunil Chander Sharma, a Civil Engineer graduate from DCE (DTU). His vision has always been to contribute to the global recruitment process, supporting the world’s growth and development. Under his visionary leadership, SEC earned recognition from the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India, ranking 3rd in India for overseas manpower recruitment.

Accompanying him in carrying this vision forward are his sons—Mr. Shivvang Sharma and Mr. Paraaj Sunil Sharma.

Mr. Shivvang Sharma began his journey at Suntech as an intern during his academic years, deeply aligned with the company’s mission of creating livelihood opportunities and supporting global workforce development. Today, as a Director of Suntech, he continues to expand the company’s horizons by strategically empowering Indian candidates while also ensuring opportunities for candidates from Nepal, Sri Lanka, Vietnam, the Philippines, and Africa, depending on employer requirements and visa quota allowances. With his strong understanding of both client expectations and candidate aspirations, he bridges opportunities that fuel growth and trust.

Mr. Paraaj Sunil Sharma, with a strong background in the mining and infrastructure sector, is now actively engaged in the operations and strategic growth of Suntech Engineers & Contractors. Under the guidance and experience of his father, he continues to amplify and expand the company’s global recruitment vision. His leadership focuses on strengthening SEC’s presence worldwide, making it a true global recruitment powerhouse.

Together, Mr. Sunil Chander Sharma, Mr. Shivvang Sharma, and Mr. Paraaj Sunil Sharma represent the perfect blend of vision, experience, growth, speed, spirit, and energy—anchored by the traditional values, ethics, and strengths that form the foundation of Suntech.

Over the past 35 years, SEC has proudly contributed to some of the most prestigious projects worldwide, including Burj Khalifa, Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, Palm Jumeirah Island, Burj Al Arab, MIG Tower, and many more, while collaborating with world-renowned organizations such as Arabian Construction Company, Urbacon Trading & Contracting, Fibrex, NPCC, and others.

Our expertise spans across a wide range of industries, including: Construction , Oil & Gas , Electromechanical , Hospitality , Industrial & Plantations , Manufacturing , Transport & Excavation , IT & Communicationsss

Why Choose Us

Connecting Talent, Creating Opportunities

We connect the right talent with the right opportunities—through personalized service and deep industry knowledge—to ensure every placement delivers real impact.

Suntech Engineers & Contractors was born out of a real-world challenge: the critical disconnect between job roles and candidate capabilities. While serving as a Project Manager in the construction industry, I witnessed firsthand how poor recruitment led to project delays, safety issues, and increased costs.

To solve this, I established Suntech in 1990, building a team of expert engineers and supervisors with deep domain knowledge across Oil & Gas, EPC, MEP, Facility Management, Civil Construction, Power & Energy, Infrastructure Development, Manufacturing, Renewable Energy, Marine & Offshore, Industrial Maintenance, Construction, Electromechanical, Hospitality, Industrial & Plantations, Transport & Excavation, and IT & Communications.

Our mission is to bridge the talent gap by identifying, screening, and deploying the right workforce with speed and precision—delivering reliability and results to businesses worldwide.

To empower skilled workers and connect them with the right opportunities while preserving the legacy of integrity, commitment, and service excellence built by our founder. Inspired by our father’s entrepreneurial spirit and guided by a technical foundation, We aim to uphold and evolve the Suntech legacy—supporting the livelihoods of thousands and becoming a trusted name in global manpower solutions.

We don’t just fill vacancies—we build teams that perform.

Through a structured recruitment process, industry-specific assessments, and a dedicated operations team, we ensure that only qualified and job-ready professionals are matched with employers.

From blue-collar trades to specialized engineers, our manpower solutions are tailored for sectors that demand skill, compliance, and efficiency.

With over three decades of experience, Suntech offers:
  • Pre-screened & tested manpower
  • End-to-End project-based staffing
  • Customized recruitment based on client needs
  • Global reach with local expertise
