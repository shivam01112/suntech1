aBOUT US

We Don’t Just Fill Roles — We Build Value

Suntech Engineers & Contractors (SEC) has been at the forefront of global workforce solutions for over three decades, driven by a clear purpose: to connect the right talent with the right opportunity and help businesses grow with confidence.

Since our inception, we have supported hundreds of clients by delivering manpower that not only fills positions but also adds measurable value to their organizations. From skilled, semi-skilled, to unskilled professionals, we provide comprehensive workforce solutions across diverse industries including Construction, Oil & Gas, MEP (Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing), Manufacturing, Facility Management, Hospitality, and more.

With a proven track record, we have successfully deployed candidates across the Middle East, Gulf countries, Europe, and Russia, contributing to the success of major projects and strengthening businesses with the right talent at the right time.

Why Us

  • Experience that delivers:With over 500,000 successful placements and a growing list of 750+ satisfied clients, our legacy speaks for itself.
  • Industry reach: We provide recruitment services for a wide range of sectors, tailoring our approach to meet the specific needs of each project.
  • End-to-End Workforce Solutions: From sourcing and screening to mobilization, visa processing, and post-deployment support — we manage the entire recruitment lifecycle.
  • Trusted by Governments and Top Employers: Our strong presence and associations across UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Bahrain, and other Gulf nations enable us to deliver compliant, timely, and high-quality recruitment services.
  • Agile & Scalable: Whether it's a large-scale deployment or a niche hiring need, our recruitment strategies are fast, flexible, and built to scale.
  • Diversity-Driven Approach: We believe that building diverse teams strengthens organizations. Our recruiters understand the cultural and operational fit that matters most.
