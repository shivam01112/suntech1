Recruitment Process

Finding the right talent is key to any successful project. But let’s face it—sourcing skilled, reliable workers isn’t always easy. At Suntech Engineers & Contractors, we understand these challenges and are here to simplify the process for you with a clear, step-by-step recruitment system that delivers results.


We follow a strategic and scalable recruitment model process step by step as mentioned below

  • Understanding client demands

    Everything starts with understanding client’s needs. Our recruitment specialists evaluate and learn about your specific workforce requirements so we can build a hiring plan that works for clients.

  • Establishing a work plan

    Once we know your needs, we make a proper plan to start the recruitment. This helps us find the right people faster and more efficiently.

  • Advertising the Job

    We post job ads in job portals, and online platforms to reach as many suitable candidates as possible.

  • Searching Our Database

    Alongside advertising, we also dig into our own rich database of pre-screened professionals and connect with those who match your criteria.

  • Screening of shortlisted applicants

    All shortlisted applicants go through a thorough screening process—including telephonic and video interviews—so you only meet the best of the best.

  • Interview procedure

    Whether it’s face-to-face at our offices or virtual interviews via Zoom, Skype, or Google Meet, we make the interview process seamless and efficient.

  • Skill Testing & Training

    We go beyond interviews. Candidates undergo skill assessments and receive hands-on training where needed—ensuring they meet international standards and are job-ready from day one.

  • Health & Medical Checks

    To make sure every candidate is fit for the job, we coordinate full medical evaluations in line with your company’s requirements.

  • Visa & Documentation Support

    Our expert visa processing team handles all paperwork swiftly and efficiently, minimizing delays and ensuring compliance.

  • Immigration clearance paperwork

    From documentation to formalities, we guide candidates through every step of the immigration process—stress-free and on time.

  • Departure of candidates

    We coordinate candidate travel and ensure a smooth arrival in the host country. From airport pickup to job site onboarding—we’ve got it covered.

  • Post-Deployment Feedback

    Once candidates are on the ground, we stay in touch. Your feedback helps us improve continuously and support your future recruitment needs even better.
